ABOUT

Who we are

Previously known as Executive Electrical Ltd since 1977, Black Cat Electrical Ltd was renamed in 2017 when long time employee, Paul Edgar, took over the ownership of the company. Black Cat Electrical Ltd has continued to supply electrical maintenance and repairs to some of New Zealand's leading businesses. Black Cat Electrical can repair and maintain your business's electrical equipment efficiently and to the highest possible standards.

about

What can we do for your company?

The team at Black Cat Electrical Ltd have extensive experience completing industrial and commercial electrical jobs, across Auckland, providing businesses with an exceptional service. Our electrical contractors provide comprehensive solutions for industries and businesses, making repairs, upgrades and installations easy and cost-effective. We can be relied on to provide solutions utilising state-of-the-art technologies and methods, and we pride ourselves on being up-to-date with the latest development in electrical technology and environmental efficiency. Black Cat Electrical Ltd is committed to providing you excellent service by registered electricians. Whether your need is for small electrical repairs or for major electrical services, you can rest easy with our 100% guarantee on all work.
